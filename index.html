How It Works

3-12 players gather around one device. Add everyone's names to begin.

Most players get the same word. The imposter gets a different one!

How to Play

Master the game in 5 simple steps

01

Create Your Game

Add 3-12 players, choose a category (Animals, Food, Movies, etc.), and set the number of imposters.

02

Get Your Role

Each player takes a turn secretly viewing their word on the screen. Tap to reveal!

03

Give Clues

Players take turns giving ONE-word clues about their secret word. Be clever but don't give it away!

04

Discussion Time

Talk with other players. Use the clues to figure out who doesn't know the real word.

05

Vote & Win

Vote to eliminate who you think is the imposter. Civilians win if all imposters are found!
